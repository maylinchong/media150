Interactive Story – “Survive 3rd Ave”

“Survive 3rd Ave” is an interactive, choice‑based story about navigating sensory overload in a crowded college environment. The experience begins on the Hunter College skybridge, a space that’s always loud, busy, and overwhelming. As the player moves through the story, they face everyday situations that can feel intense when the world becomes too fast or too bright — rushing crowds, overstimulation, disconnection, and pressure to keep up.

The player chooses small grounding tools — a deep breath, sunglasses, stepping aside, taking off headphones — and each choice shapes the emotional outcome. The story isn’t about “winning”; it’s about recognizing how small decisions can help someone regain control in a stressful moment. The project uses video backgrounds, soft fade‑in text, and simple visuals to create an immersive atmosphere that mirrors the feeling of navigating a crowded campus while trying to stay centered.
