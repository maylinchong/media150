Depth of Field Exercises

Shallow Depth of Field

This image shows shallow depth of field because only the hands and the camera are in focus, while the background is blurry. The blur helps bring attention to the subject and makes the moment feel more personal and focused.

Deep Depth of Field

This image shows deep depth of field because most of the objects in the photo, like the drink, flowers, and background items, are clear and in focus. This allows the viewer to see all the details in the scene instead of focusing on just one subject.
